OV.Seva - Online Voting Seva

Description

OV.Seva or Online Voting Seva is a Blockchain based online voting system. Wherein we as creators have provided a voting system, wherein every user can regsister himself/herself as either a voter or a candidate and can cast vote to a candidate according to their choice after thorough verification of their details from the back-end server, storing the data.

OV.Seva would allow each and every user to cast their vote fairly and securely, as Blockchain technology is the backbone of this whole system. Blockchain allows the user/client to work upon events securely as it stores the data in the form of blocks containing three major portions which are:
1) Time Stamp of the block (when was the block created)
2) Data to be stored (E.g. Transaction data, Voting data)
3) Hash of the previous block - making it impossible to hack or crackdown

Advantages

1) Each and every person can cast their vote easily, securely, anytime and anywhere.
2) Need not to wait in long queues for voting, thus time-saving system.
3)It has great potential to decrease organizational costs and increase voter turnout. It eliminates the need to print ballot papers or open polling stations-voters can vote from wherever there is an Internet connection.
